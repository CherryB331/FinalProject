Explore Salem College and the Surrounding Area

Campus Life

Students are the driving force behind the vibrant campus community.

  • Campus Events: Clubs and organizations host various events throughout the year to engage students and foster a sense of community.
  • Student Led: Clubs are student-run and provide opportunities for leadership development and civic engagement.
  • Recreational Activities: Salem offers recreational activities and facilities for students to stay active and have fun on campus.

The city of Winston-Salem

Winston-Salem is a college town that offers city events for all students.

  • Old Salem: A historic district with shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions. Right next to campus.
  • Cafes: There are several cafes throughout the city where students can meet and socialize. It will be easy to find your favorite spot.
  • Events: You will find various events and festivals throughout the year that celebrate the culture and diversity of the community.
