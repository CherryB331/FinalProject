Resources at Salem College

Academic Resources

Salem offers a wide range of academic resources to support your learning and development.

  • Library: Access a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support your research and studies.
    • Study Spaces: The library provides quiet study areas, group study rooms, and computer labs for students to work on assignments and projects.
    • Quest Center: Get help with your coursework through one-on-one tutoring sessions in various subjects.
    • Writing Center: Improve your writing skills with the assistance of experienced tutors who can help you with essays, research papers, and more.
    • Rentals: Access to laptops and other electronic devices for students. Textbooks for various courses are also available.
  • Academic Advising: Receive guidance on course selection, degree planning, and career advice from knowledgeable advisors.

Personal Resources

Salem provides various personal resources to support your well-being and mental health.

  • Counseling Services: Access confidential counseling and support for mental health concerns, stress management, and personal development.
  • Health Services: Receive medical care and health education to maintain your physical well-being while on campus.
  • Student Support Services: Get assistance with financial aid, housing, and other resources to help you navigate college life successfully.
