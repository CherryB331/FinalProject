Possibilities at Salem College

Internship and CareerOpportunities

Salem helps train students to be prepared for their future careers.

  • Lucy Rose Center: A dedicated space for career development and job search assistance.
    • Resume Writing: Get help with creating and improving your resume.
    • Mock Interviews: Practice your interviewing skills with simulated interviews.
    • Internship Programs Research: Salem looks intovarious internship opportunities in different fields to provide students with real-world experience and enhance their resumes.
    • Career Fairs: Regularly hosted career fairs where students can connect with potential employers and learn about job opportunities.

Opportunities after graduation

Salem provides various personal resources to support your well-being and mental health.

  • Lucy Rose Center: A dedicated space for career development and job search assistance.
    • GRE Preparation: Get help with preparing for the Graduate Record Examination.
    • Grad School Research: Look into all graduate schools to see what fits best.
  • Grad Advising:Salem offers accelerated degree programs for students who wish to pursue advanced degrees. Academic advisors help guide them through that pathway.
